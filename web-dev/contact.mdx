EducaPerú responds to every web development inquiry with a professional proposal within 24 hours. The blueprint is completely free and comes with no obligation — you review and approve everything before any work begins. Share the details of your project today and take the first step toward a website that works as hard as you do.

## What to Include in Your Request

Providing these three pieces of information ensures EducaPerú can prepare an accurate and tailored proposal for your project.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Your Name or Company Name">
    Let us know who we are building for — whether you are an individual entrepreneur or representing an established business in Perú.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your Email Address">
    A valid email address where EducaPerú can deliver your proposal within the 24-hour window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="A Brief Project Description">
    Describe what your business does, what you want to achieve with the website, any target audience details, and any existing brand guidelines or visual references you already have. The more context you provide, the more precise and useful the blueprint will be.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Happens Next

Once your request is submitted, EducaPerú follows a clear and prompt process to get your blueprint into your hands quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request Review">
    The EducaPerú team reviews your submission within a few hours of receipt, evaluating your project scope and objectives.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Blueprint Delivery">
    You receive a professional proposal — including a visual mockup and proposed site architecture — within 24 hours of your initial inquiry.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your Review and Approval">
    Take all the time you need to review the blueprint. No development begins until you have reviewed the proposal and confirmed you are happy to proceed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Development Kickoff">
    Once approved, development starts according to the confirmed timeline you and the team have agreed on together.
  </Step>
</Steps>
