## Contact Form Fields

The inquiry form on the EducaPerú web development page collects three pieces of information to prepare an accurate, tailored proposal.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Your Name or Company Name">
    A text field for your name or business name — whether you are an individual entrepreneur or representing an established company in Perú.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your Email Address">
    A valid email address where EducaPerú can deliver your proposal within the 24-hour window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Project Description">
    A textarea to describe your project briefly — what your business does, what you want to achieve with the website, and any other relevant context. The more detail you provide, the more precise and useful the blueprint will be.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Completed forms are submitted via the **"Solicitar Blueprint Gratuito"** button. EducaPerú commits to delivering a professional proposal within 24 hours of receipt.
