EducaPerú follows a disciplined four-phase roadmap — **Nuestra Hoja de Ruta** — designed to eliminate uncertainty and deliver predictable outcomes. Each phase ends with a clear milestone before the next begins, ensuring you remain in full control throughout the project. Structured processes mean fewer surprises, faster delivery, and a final product that meets your goals precisely.

## The Four Phases

<Steps>
  <Step title="Discovery">
    A thorough audit of your business paired with clear digital goal definition. Before a single line of code is written, EducaPerú takes the time to understand your brand identity, target audience, competitive landscape, and measurable objectives — so the entire project is built on a solid strategic foundation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Proposal">
    A complete visual design and site architecture delivered for your review and approval. You see exactly how the site will look and how content will be structured before development begins. Nothing moves forward until you are fully satisfied with the direction.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Development">
    Construction of the approved design using modern web technologies, with SEO optimization integrated throughout. The result is a responsive, fast-loading, and accessible website that performs well on both search engines and any device your visitors use.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch">
    Final quality assurance testing, production deployment, and go-live confirmation. After launch, EducaPerú continues to monitor and support your site — ensuring stability, security, and performance over the long term.
  </Step>
</Steps>
