EducaPerú follows a disciplined four-phase roadmap — **Nuestra Hoja de Ruta** — designed to eliminate uncertainty and deliver predictable outcomes. Each phase ends with a clear milestone before the next begins, ensuring you remain in full control throughout the project. Structured processes mean fewer surprises, faster delivery, and a final product that meets your goals precisely.

## The Four Phases

<Steps>
  <Step title="01 — Descubrimiento">
    Auditoría de tu negocio y definición de objetivos claros. Before a single line of code is written, EducaPerú audits your business and defines clear digital goals — understanding your brand, audience, and measurable objectives so the entire project is built on a solid strategic foundation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="02 — Propuesta">
    Diseño visual y arquitectura del sitio bajo tu aprobación. A complete visual design and site architecture is delivered for your review. Nothing moves forward until you are fully satisfied with the direction.
  </Step>
  <Step title="03 — Desarrollo">
    Construcción con tecnologías modernas y optimización SEO. The approved design is built using modern web technologies with SEO optimization integrated throughout — producing a responsive, fast-loading site that performs well on every device and search engine.
  </Step>
  <Step title="04 — Lanzamiento">
    Pruebas finales, puesta en marcha y soporte continuo. Final quality assurance testing, production deployment, and go-live confirmation. EducaPerú continues to support your site after launch to ensure stability, security, and performance over the long term.
  </Step>
</Steps>
