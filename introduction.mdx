EducaPerú (EDUCA PERÚ S.A.C., RUC 20615550117) is a Peruvian training center that prepares students, career starters, and working professionals with the technical skills that today's job market demands. Through specialised programs in office productivity and programming — delivered entirely online with live advisory sessions — EducaPerú bridges the gap between classroom theory and real workplace requirements.

## Platform Highlights

- **32 academic hours per course** — structured weekly sessions of 4 hours, covering a full curriculum from fundamentals to applied practice.
- **Certified instructors with downloadable CVs** — every course is taught by a verified specialist; you can download instructor CVs directly from the course detail page before enrolling.
- **Online + advisory modality** — lessons are delivered online with dedicated advisory sessions so you can ask questions and get personalised guidance throughout the program.
- **Lifetime access to course materials** — once enrolled, your access to lessons and resources never expires, including any future updates to the curriculum.
- **Certificate of completion included** — a formal certificate is issued upon finishing the course, ready to add to your professional portfolio.

  The **Ofimática Profesional** course is currently available for enrolment at a promotional price of **S/ 120** (regular price S/ 250). Courses in **C++ Programming** and **Python Programming** are under development and will be released soon.
