EducaPerú (Centro de Capacitación y Consultoría Empresarial EDUCA PERÚ S.A.C.) is a Peruvian platform dedicated to professional technology education and web development services. Whether you want to master Microsoft Office tools, learn programming from scratch, or get a polished website for your business, EducaPerú provides structured programs taught by active industry professionals.
