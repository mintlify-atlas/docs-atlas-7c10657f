At EducaPerú, values are not a wall decoration — they are the operating principles that shape every program design decision, every instructor interaction, and every student experience. These five core values define what the institution stands for and set the standard to which every member of the EducaPerú community is held, from academic coordinators to frontline instructors to enrolled students.

## Core Institutional Values

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Excelencia Académica" icon="award" href="/about/mission-vision">
    EducaPerú maintains high standards of academic quality in every program it delivers. Content is continuously updated to remain pertinent, rigorous, and aligned with real professional demands.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Responsabilidad" icon="shield-check" href="/about/overview">
    Commitment, seriousness, and consistent follow-through define how EducaPerú operates — in both academic processes and administrative matters. Students and partners can always count on the institution to honor its word.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Ética Profesional" icon="heart-handshake" href="/about/overview">
    Transparency, integrity, and mutual respect govern every relationship at EducaPerú — toward students, collaborators, and partner organizations alike. Ethical conduct is expected at every level of the institution.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Innovación" icon="lightbulb" href="/courses/catalog">
    EducaPerú embraces modern pedagogical methodologies and current technological tools to ensure that every program delivers real, measurable value — not just theoretical knowledge.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Vocación de Servicio" icon="hand-pointer" href="/about/mission-vision">
    Timely accompaniment and attentive support are central to the EducaPerú experience. The institution prioritizes its academic community, ensuring that students and collaborators always have access to the guidance they need, when they need it.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  These five values are directly reflected in the way EducaPerú designs its curricula and selects its instructors. Every course structure is reviewed against these principles, and every instructor is evaluated not only on technical expertise but on their commitment to ethics, innovation, and genuine service to students.
</Note>
