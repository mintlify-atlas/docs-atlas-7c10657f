Every program, course, and consultancy engagement at EducaPerú is guided by a clear institutional north star: combine **academic excellence**, **methodological innovation**, and **permanent support** to produce professionals who make a measurable difference in their organizations. This directional clarity — expressed through the institution's mission and vision — shapes how curricula are designed, how instructors are selected, and how students are accompanied throughout their learning journey.

## Mission and Vision

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Misión">
    EducaPerú's mission defines the day-to-day commitment the institution makes to every student and partner organization:

    > Brindar programas de capacitación, especialización y consultoría empresarial con altos estándares de calidad académica, orientados al desarrollo de competencias estratégicas, la actualización profesional continua y la aplicación práctica del conocimiento, contribuyendo al fortalecimiento del desempeño laboral y al crecimiento sostenible de las organizaciones.

    In practice, this means delivering programs in *capacitación* (training), *especialización* (specialization), and business consultancy that meet rigorous academic standards — always oriented toward strategic competency development, continuous professional updating, and the practical application of knowledge. The ultimate goal is to strengthen individual job performance while contributing to the sustainable growth of the organizations these professionals serve.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Visión">
    EducaPerú's vision articulates the long-term institutional aspiration:

    > Consolidarnos como una institución referente a nivel nacional en formación especializada y consultoría empresarial, reconocida por su excelencia académica, innovación metodológica y compromiso permanente con el desarrollo profesional y la mejora continua.

    The institution aims to establish itself as the national benchmark in specialized education and business consultancy in Peru — recognized not only for academic excellence and methodological innovation, but above all for an unwavering, permanent commitment to professional development and continuous improvement at every level of the organization.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
