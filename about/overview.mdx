**Centro de Capacitación y Consultoría Empresarial EDUCA PERÚ S.A.C.** (RUC 20615550117) is a Peruvian institution committed to active contribution to the professional development of those seeking to grow, innovate, and stand out in an increasingly competitive and dynamic environment. EducaPerú offers specialized training programs and business consultancy services, combining rigorous academic standards with hands-on methodology. Whether through structured certification courses or practical web development programs, every offering is designed to produce professionals who are ready to contribute from day one.

> *"La preparación de hoy es el éxito de mañana."*

## Our Story

EducaPerú's Specialization Programs were launched in direct response to the need for updated, practical training aligned with the real demands of the job market. The institution firmly believes that continuous education is the fundamental pillar for strengthening competencies, developing talent, and generating new professional opportunities. This conviction shapes every program EducaPerú designs and every instructor it selects.

The institutional approach combines academic excellence, methodological innovation, and permanent accompaniment — guaranteeing a formative experience that goes beyond the theoretical and translates into concrete results. Every participant is invited to take on this challenge with commitment and a vision for the future. At EDUCA PERÚ, each step in a student's formation represents a solid advance in their professional development.
