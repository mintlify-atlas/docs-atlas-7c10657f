**Centro de Capacitación y Consultoría Empresarial EDUCA PERÚ S.A.C.** (RUC 20615550117) is a Peruvian institution founded to bridge the gap between formal education and the real demands of today's job market. EducaPerú offers specialized training programs and business consultancy services, combining rigorous academic standards with hands-on methodology. Whether through structured certification courses or practical web development programs, every offering is designed to produce professionals who are ready to contribute from day one.

> *"La preparación de hoy es el éxito de mañana."*

## Our Story

EducaPerú was built on a clear conviction: professional training must be relevant, practical, and continuously evolving. From its founding, the institution has pursued a dual mission — delivering high-quality academic programs and supporting organizations through business consultancy. This integrated approach reflects EducaPerú's belief that individual growth and organizational strength are deeply connected.

> Brindar programas de capacitación, especialización y consultoría empresarial con altos estándares de calidad académica, orientados al desarrollo de competencias estratégicas, la actualización profesional continua y la aplicación práctica del conocimiento, contribuyendo al fortalecimiento del desempeño laboral y al crecimiento sostenible de las organizaciones.
