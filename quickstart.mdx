EducaPerú serves two distinct needs: structured technical training through its course catalog, and custom web development for businesses. This quickstart covers both paths — follow the **Enroll in a Course** tab if you want to learn Ofimática Profesional (or any upcoming program), or switch to the **Request a Web Project** tab if you need a professionally built website or application for your company.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Enroll in a Course">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Browse the course catalog">
        Navigate to the [course catalog](/courses/catalog) to see all available and upcoming programs. The catalog currently lists **Ofimática Profesional** (available now), along with **C++ Programming** and **Python Programming** (coming soon). Each card shows the weekly time commitment and a direct link to the full course detail page.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Ofimática Profesional">
        Click the **Ofimática Profesional** card to open the [course detail page](/courses/ofimatica). Review the full program breakdown:

        | Detail | Value |
        |---|---|
        | Promotional price | **S/ 120** |
        | Regular price | ~~S/ 250~~ |
        | Duration | 32 academic hours |
        | Lessons | 24 practical lessons |
        | Weekly pace | 4 hours per week |
        | Modality | Online + advisory sessions |
        | Certificate | Included |

        You can also download the instructor CVs for **Deysi A. Flores Toledo** and **Roxana K. Diaz Zavala** directly from this page to verify credentials before enrolling.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click 'Comenzar Ahora' and log in">
        Press the **Comenzar Ahora** button on the course detail page. You will be redirected to the student login portal at `/login`. Enter your credentials to complete the enrolment process.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Access your lessons and materials">
        Once enrolled, you have immediate access to all **24 practical lessons** and the full set of course materials. Work through sessions at your own pace — 4 hours per week is the recommended schedule to complete the course in 2–3 months. Lifetime access to all materials and future curriculum updates is included.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
      Lifetime access to all course materials and future curriculum updates is included with every enrolment — you never lose access after the course ends.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Request a Web Project">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Review the web development services">
        Visit the [web development services page](/web-dev/overview) to learn about EducaPerú's web development offerings. The page outlines the project phases — Discovery, Proposal, Development, and Launch — and the types of solutions the team builds, so you can confirm the service is the right fit before reaching out.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Fill out the contact form">
        Scroll to the contact section and complete the project enquiry form. Provide the following information so the team can prepare an accurate proposal:

        - **Name / Company** — your full name or business name
        - **Email address** — the address where you want to receive the proposal
        - **Project description** — a brief summary of what you need for your project
      </Step>
      <Step title="Receive a professional proposal within 24 hours">
        After submitting the form, the EducaPerú development team will review your requirements and send a professional proposal to your email within 24 hours.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click 'Solicitar Blueprint Gratuito' to submit">
        Press the **Solicitar Blueprint Gratuito** button to send your enquiry. Once the team reviews your submission, they will follow up according to the four-phase roadmap: Discovery → Proposal → Development → Launch.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  **C++ Programming** and **Python Programming** courses are coming soon. Check the [course catalog](/courses/catalog) regularly for updates on availability and enrolment dates.
</Note>
