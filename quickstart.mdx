EducaPerú serves two distinct needs: structured technical training through its course catalog, and custom web development for businesses. This quickstart covers both paths — follow the **Enroll in a Course** tab if you want to learn Ofimática Profesional (or any upcoming program), or switch to the **Request a Web Project** tab if you need a professionally built website or application for your company.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Enroll in a Course">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Browse the course catalog">
        Navigate to the [course catalog](/courses/catalog) to see all available and upcoming programs. The catalog currently lists **Ofimática Profesional** (available now), along with **C++ Programming** and **Python Programming** (coming soon). Each card shows the weekly time commitment and a direct link to the full course detail page.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Ofimática Profesional">
        Click the **Ofimática Profesional** card to open the [course detail page](/courses/ofimatica). Review the full program breakdown:

        | Detail | Value |
        |---|---|
        | Promotional price | **S/ 120** |
        | Regular price | ~~S/ 250~~ |
        | Duration | 32 academic hours |
        | Lessons | 24 practical lessons |
        | Modality | Online + advisory sessions |
        | Certificate | Included |

        You can also download the instructor CVs directly from this page to verify credentials before enrolling.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click 'Comenzar Ahora' and log in">
        Press the **Comenzar Ahora** button on the course detail page. You will be redirected to the student login portal (`/login` on the live site). Enter your credentials to access your student dashboard and complete the enrolment process.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Access your lessons and materials">
        Once enrolled, your dashboard gives you immediate access to all **24 practical lessons** and the full set of course materials. Work through sessions at your own pace — 4 hours per week is the recommended schedule to complete the course in 2–3 months.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
      Lifetime access to all course materials and future curriculum updates is included with every enrolment — you never lose access after the course ends.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Request a Web Project">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Review the web development services">
        Visit the [web development services page](/web-dev/overview) to explore EducaPerú's web development offerings. The page outlines the types of projects the team handles, the technologies used, and examples of previous work, so you can confirm the service is the right fit before reaching out.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Fill out the contact form">
        Complete the project enquiry form on the page. Provide the following information so the team can prepare an accurate proposal:

        - **Name / Company name** — your full name or the legal name of your business
        - **Email address** — the address where you want to receive the proposal
        - **Project description** — a clear summary of what you need: site type, key features, target audience, and any design references
      </Step>
      <Step title="Receive a professional proposal">
        After submitting the form, the EducaPerú development team will review your requirements and send a detailed, professional proposal to your email **within 24 hours**. The proposal includes scope, technology stack, timeline, and pricing.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Review and approve the project blueprint">
        Go through the proposal with your team. Once you are satisfied with the scope and terms, confirm your approval to kick off the project. The EducaPerú team will then schedule a kickoff call and begin development according to the agreed blueprint.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  **C++ Programming** and **Python Programming** courses are coming soon. Check the [course catalog](/courses/catalog) regularly for updates on availability and enrolment dates.
</Note>
